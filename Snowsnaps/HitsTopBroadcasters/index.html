SNOWSNAPS comes to the small screen as part of a well-established, highly acclaimed and successful Canadian Brand and Intellectual Property – LA GUERRE DES TUQUES 3D / SNOWTIME! This animated feature film was the highest grossing Canadian film in 2015, it has been sold to every market in the world, was featured at the Sundance Film Festival, and was in official competition in Annecy. With 11 weeks in theatres in France and 6,5 M Box Office in China, SNOWTIME! was officially an international success.

Each episode of SNOWSNAPS is driven by action, and the values of Friendship, collaboration, determination and proudness between the four friends are subjects addressed in SNOWSNAPS through these four rascals adventures. The Series has broad appeal, plenty of laugh-out-loud humour, real heart, and four stars who are everso huggable! It is an irresistible programming combination, ripe for co-viewing and Promotes the fun of outdoor play and the discovery of new things to do particularly in the winter.

« When launching the brand, we've engaged with more than 35 000 kids trough outdoor events we've created. We unfortunately discovered that a lot of them lost the capacity to play on their own. Creating Snowsnaps was for us an opportunity to motivate kids to imagine game play in their own lives. »

Marie-Claude Beauchamp, producer

CarpeDiem Film & TV and Pink Parrot Media is happy to present the SNOWSNAPS Series, a Wintertime Outdoor Comedy of 52 animated tv episodes. SNOWSNAPS has a very specific Recipe: Take four very different little kids, open the gate to the great outdoors, cover everything with snow, and let ‘em go! In each episode the four stars of SNOWSNAPS are confronted with a challenge. Overcoming the challenge becomes a game, but the game always gets totally out of hand and leads to a surprise ending. Each characters have their own distinct nature based on comic archetypes: Violet the Scoundrel, Tomas the Innocent, Sami the Fool, and Kiki the Soldier. Putting any two of these classic comedy types together instantly creates friction and funny – with four, it’s even more!

SNOWSNAPS Series is sold world wide by Pink Perrot Media, an international sales agent that specializes in animated and live-action family entertainment. The purpose of this company is to give producers a valued and comprehensive opportunity where they feel comfortable and convinced that their projects are considered, promoted with passion and handled on a personal basis.
